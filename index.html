Overview of Real Estate Market

By Reza Rehman, Cedric Liu, Arnav Khanna

In this website, we will discuss an analysis of the real estate market based on trends from a dataset. The real estate market is constantly evolving, influenced by factors such as location, economic conditions, and buyer demand. Our data revealed that home prices tend to increase in high-demand urban areas, while suburban and rural markets offer more affordable options. We also observed seasonal price variations, with higher sales activity during spring and summer months.
